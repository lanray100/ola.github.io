Basic Capital provides real estate investment capital in a variety of forms to meet the needs of the development community and investors seeking real estate exposure with returns suitable for the level of risk. Basic Capital works with developers to provide capital solutions to project-specific and long-term capital needs, but we also initiate real estate developments on behalf of investors.

Basic Capital funds are aligned to real estate markets in second and third tier markets in Nigeria. These funds seek to capture the desirable returns available in these markets and do not rely on extensive property appreciation and timely liquidation that are required in many cities.

Our funds also seek alignment with current trends in real estate development. We focus on geographies, properties and assets that are associated with millennial attraction. We also focus on emerging industries and asset classes. We are particularly attuned to opportunities leveraging our expertise in Nigeria, and we actively seek real estate investments that leverage a national context.

For more about our fund strategies, see the strategy section of our web site.
